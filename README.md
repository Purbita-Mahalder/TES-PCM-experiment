Motivation

PCMs exhibit high latent heat capacity but suffer from very low thermal conductivity, leading to slow charge/discharge rates.
Embedding metal tubes into PCM tanks enhances heat transfer, but the trade-off between: PCM volume, heat-transfer area, tube material cost, and geometric configuration is not well understood due to a lack of systematic experimental comparisons under identical conditions.

This study addresses that gap.
